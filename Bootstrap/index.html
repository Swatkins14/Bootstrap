Favourite TV Shows

A Guide to the who/what/when/where and why of my favourite TV shows


Doctor Who

The tales of a alien called The Doctor and his adventures in time and space.

  • 2005 - Current
  • Showrunners: Russell T Davies, Steven Moffatt, Chris Chibnall
  • BBC
Bojack Horseman

Washed-up TV star, Bojack Horseman, goes through the misfortunes of life and the bad choices made in response to them.

  • 2014 - 2020
  • Created by Raphael Bob-Waksberg
  • Netflix
Fleabag

A woman, known only as Fleabag, has no filter as she navigates life and love in London while trying to cope with tragedy.

  • 2016 - 2019
  • Created by Phoebe Waller-Bridge
  • BBC
